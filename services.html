We host an expansive suite of programs to cover newcomer integration, professional growth, youth development, and cultural expression. Search or filter through our current programs below.

Housing Assistance

Providing crucial settlement guidance to help newly arrived families find safe, affordable, and welcoming housing in the capital region, including application reviews and translation help.
