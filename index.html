The Afghan Canadian Capital Society (ACCS) is a non-profit, independent community organization dedicated to establishing a welcoming home for newcomers, empowering youth, honoring seniors, and celebrating the vibrant Afghan culture in Canada.

Our Vision

Cultivating Strength in Unity

We channel collective effort and community spirit to foster a supportive landscape that nurtures Afghan identity and opens pathways of opportunity in Canada.

Supporting Integration

Providing direct community guidance, housing support, employment help, and practical workshops to ease the settlement journey for newcomers and families.

Learn More

Bridging Generations

Creating active programs that bring elders, youth, and families together to share knowledge, wisdom, and mutual support across the community.

Learn More

Cultural Continuity

Preserving our mother tongue languages, fine arts, traditional music, and celebrating national holidays to keep our rich cultural heritage alive.
